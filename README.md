### 📌 Project Overview

This project involves analyzing sales data using Excel to understand customer behavior, product performance, and business trends. The goal was to transform raw data into meaningful insights through an interactive dashboard.

### 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Data Cleaning Techniques
- Data Visualization (Charts & Dashboard Design)



### 📂 Dataset
- Sales transaction data (orders, quantity, revenue, customer details)


### 📈 Key Insights
- Sales fluctuated across 13 weeks, with peak performance in the middle period
- Customers purchasing 2–3 items formed the largest segment
- T-shirts, Jeans, and Sneakers were the top-selling products
- Most purchases were made through digital channels (Apps & Websites)
- Delivery time was mostly within 2–3 days

### 📊 Business Context
The analysis suggests that customer purchasing behavior is centered around mid-range quantity orders (2–3 items), indicating opportunities for bundled promotions. 

Top-performing products such as T-shirts and Jeans highlight strong demand in casual wear categories, which can guide inventory and marketing strategies.

The dominance of app and website purchases shows a strong preference for digital channels, emphasizing the importance of maintaining a seamless online shopping experience.

### 📌 Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts on top-performing product categories like T-shirts and Jeans  
- Introduce bundle offers targeting customers who typically purchase 2–3 items  
- Optimize delivery processes to maintain or improve the 2–3 day delivery window  
- Invest in improving app and website user experience to boost online sales  

### 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI summary (Orders, Quantity, Revenue, Rating, Delivery Time)
- Sales trend over time
- Customer purchase behavior analysis
- Product performance breakdown
- Geographic distribution of customers
- Shipment duration analysis
